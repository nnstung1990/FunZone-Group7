SUN WORLD DANANG WONDERS – A NEW WONDER OF ENDLESS EMOTIONS

Aiming to commence on a world-class amusement complex of Vietnam, Sun Group opted for Danang – a coastal tourist city on the narrow Central swathe of Vietnam to be the site for a great amusement and entertainment park by the name of Sun World Danang Wonders.

Spanning a total area of 868,694sqm on the western bank of Han River, Sun World Danang Wonders amusement park blends a string of unique and unseen features of entertainment models of the world and an ubiquitous touch of Oriental culture. Sun World Danang Wonders comprises three major zones: a modern outdoor park, a cultural park that cosists of mini architectural works and artworks to symbolize 10 Asian countries, and the Sun Wheel – a hybrid ensemble of the old and the new.

THE LEGENDARY PLACE

A famous tourist attraction in Danang

Sun World Danang Wonders Amusement Park offers a series of unique games that are adopted for the first time to Vietnam. Best loved favorite games include roller coaster, freefall tower and hi-speed slide… are expected to lavish you all with most exciting experiences.

The cultural park is a reenactment of the Oriental realm through replicas of diverse cultural landmarks, architectural and historical sites, art performances and quintessential cuisines of 10 Asian countries, namely Japan, Indonesia, Singapore, Korea, India, Nepal, Thailand, Cambodia, China and Vietnam.

DIVERSE CUISINE

Local and ubiquitously Asian restaurants will not disappoint die-hard foodies with perfect treats for your energetic and vibrant trip in Sun World Danang Wonders.
