Welcome to Park Character



Barysan

Barysan is the mascot of Imabari city, Ehime prefecture which is famous for its yakitori (grilled chicken skewers) and senzanki (fried chicken).

Seito-kun

Sento-kun is the mascot that attracts many tourists to Nara city. Nara city is also recognized by Unesco as a World Heritage Site with many of the oldest temples in Japan.

Choruru

Choruru is the iconic tourist mascot of Yamaguchi Prefecture, famous for the Kintai Bridge. This is also one of the ideal places to see cherry blossoms in spring.

Gunma-chan

Gunma-chan is the iconic mascot of Gunma Prefecture..

Shimanekko

Shimanekko is a cute cat, a Shimane prefectural tourist icon with an image of the Izumo Taisha shrine above her head.

Hikonyan

Hikonyan is the tourist symbol of Hikone city, a cute cat with a samurai hat

Kumamon

Kumamon is the mascot of Kumamoto city - a chubby black bear with red cheeks.

PinkFong

Pinkfong is the name of a children's educational brand of SmartStudy, a Korean education and entertainment company, a subsidiary of Samsung Publishing Co Ltd.
