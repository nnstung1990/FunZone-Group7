Lantern Festival

Officially held between June 10 and September 3, Lantern Festival as entitled “Childhood dreams” will lull participants in the fantasy of a wonderland in which they traverse remotest and mos mysterious planets in fairy tales, to spectacular scenes in sci-fi films and even the realm of comics that have fascinated many generations. The first planet of this journey is the world of giant creatures, including mysterious Cheshire cat, Absolem butterflies and flamboyant and beaming mushrooms. Stopping by the “planet of giant creatures” while the drifting dream still lingers on adventurous hearts, astronauts in fairylike lantern world continued to be lost in the “Dinosaur Planet”. Fans of superhero saga “The Avengers” should not miss out on the “Superhero Planet”, a commonplace of Spider Man, Batman, Iron Man, Hulk, to be followed by “Pokémon Planet”, with lanterns modeled after adorable and laughable characters in Pokémon seris and Pokémon Go game that captured the fantasy of youngsters such as Pikachu or Satoshi…
