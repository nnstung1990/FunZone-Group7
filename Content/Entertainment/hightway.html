Highway Boat

Idea: Inspired by the pirate chase of the Malacca region (Singapore), the game will bring extreme excitement when tumbling in the air.

Parameter:
Minimum height to be allowed to join the game: 120cm
Children from 120 -132 cm must be accompanied by an adult
Track length: 680km
Velocity: 50km/h
