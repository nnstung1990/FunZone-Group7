What's Up --> Ice cream


Ice cream week

In 8 consecutive days from August 20 to 27, Sun World Danang Wonders hosted the super attractive “Ice Cream Week”. In addition to free access to signature French ice cream, visitors are also entitled to best and most fabulous activities related to ice cream, the Lantern Festival and many other great activities.
In particular, in 4 consecutive days from August 24 to 27, entry ticket fare to Sun World Danang Wonders is just VND50,000/adult and free for children below 1m tall, so that visitors can both enjoy premium French ice cream and roam the amusement complex out loud. Moreover, by buying one entry ticket, visitors are offered with 01 lucky draw ticket to be among winners of trendy OPPO F1s smartphones, stylish drawn suitcases or other free ice cream vouchers.
